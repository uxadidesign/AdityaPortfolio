1. Project Overview

Background & Business Context

Finflow is a cloud-based invoice management platform helping businesses configure billing, process corporate tax filings, and track payables.

The existing system was slow, leading to high billing calculation errors and manual correction cycles.

Goals & Objectives:

  • Minimize billing processing delays by 25%.
  • Simplify recurring payment setup workflows.
  • Encourage clean reporting logs for audit trials.

2. My Role & Collaboration

As the Lead UI/UX Designer, I owned layout structures, interactive spreadsheets, automated calculations, and developer handoffs.

3. Research & Discovery

We interviewed 12 finance managers, learning they spent half their time reconciling local tax percentages manually due to static form layouts.

9. Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Inaccurate tax entries

The Problem: Corporate users calculated tax fields incorrectly due to varying state laws, stalling payments.

Design Solution: We added an auto-lookup selector that computes calculations inline, reducing billing errors.
