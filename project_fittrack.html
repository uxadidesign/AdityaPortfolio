1. Project Overview

Background & Business Context

Fittrack is a fitness companion app connecting to smartwatches. The primary goal was to make logging workouts fun and interactive, encouraging users to meet daily active goals.

The previous interface used simple listing tables, causing high user churn within two weeks.

Goals & Objectives:

  • Increase daily active user engagement by 18%.
  • Improve workout log completion rates.
  • Integrate social challenge elements.

2. My Role & Collaboration

As the Lead Mobile Designer, I owned end-to-end user flows, companion smartwatch mockups, and interactive workout graphs.

3. Research & Discovery

We ran remote usability tests, learning that users abandoned logs because the workout selector had 15+ sub-folders, making logging slow while exercising.

4. Problem Definition

+18% Target DAU engagement
+22% Target completion rate

5. Design Process

We designed a flat hierarchy layout with quick-log triggers and activity rings.

6. Visual Design System

We established vibrant neon and black layout styling variables for high visibility under outdoor sunlight conditions.

7. High-Fidelity Walkthrough

Workout Progress Ring

A tactile, interactive progress dashboard showing calorie burns and step tracking targets that syncs instantly.

8. Product Strategy

We introduced gamified badges and group challenges to increase user retention rates.

9. Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Inaccurate calorie metrics

The Problem: Users complained that the companion smartwatch app showed calorie count calculations that differed from their exercise equipment.

Design Solution: We created a calibration step mapping and clear tooltips explaining the data source logic, building user trust.
