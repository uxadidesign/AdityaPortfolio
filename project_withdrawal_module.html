Withdrawal Module

A rebuild of Bigul's fund-withdrawal flow — the single most trust-sensitive moment in a trading app — replacing a silent black box with real-time status tracking.

1. Overview

Nothing tests a trading platform's trustworthiness quite like a user asking for their money back. The Withdrawal Module rebuild focused entirely on that single moment — turning a silent, multi-day black box into a transparent, trackable process users could actually follow.

2. Problem

Once a withdrawal request was submitted, the app showed nothing further until the money arrived — no indication of which stage it was in, no clear expectation for how long banking rails would take, and no distinction between "still processing normally" and "something went wrong."

This silence was disproportionately anxiety-inducing for larger withdrawal amounts, and it was the single largest driver of repeat support contact — users would submit one request, then message support two or three times asking for an update on the exact same transaction.

3. Solution

I redesigned the flow around a visible, real-time status tracker with four clear stages, paired with an upfront, honest SLA estimate set at the moment of submission rather than a vague "we'll process it soon."

Fig 1. Real-time withdrawal status tracker replacing the previous silent confirmation screen.

4. My Role

I owned the withdrawal flow redesign end-to-end, working with the Payments/Banking Ops team to understand exactly which processing stages were technically knowable in real time, and with Support to identify the specific uncertainty moments driving repeat contact.

5. Key Features

Four-Stage Status Tracker

Requested → Processing → Bank Transfer Initiated → Completed, each with a timestamp, replacing the previous single "Submitted" confirmation with no further updates.

Upfront SLA Transparency

A realistic estimate ("Typically 1-2 business days, may take longer around bank holidays") shown at submission, not buried in a help article users had to go looking for.

Saved & Verified Bank Accounts

A quick-withdraw shortcut to previously verified accounts, removing repeated manual entry while keeping a clear audit trail of which account funds were sent to.

6. Business Impact

-24% Withdrawal Tickets
Higher Post-Withdrawal Trust Rating
Fewer Repeat Follow-Ups Per Ticket
