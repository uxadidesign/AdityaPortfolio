1. Project Overview

Background & Business Context

Novapay is a mobile neobanking startup focused on making personal finance easy for young adults. Traditional bank accounts feel static and disconnected, forcing users to use separate apps for budgeting, group expenses, and investments.

Our goal was to design an all-in-one financial app that simplifies transfers, visualizes savings goals, and automates micro-investments without cognitive fatigue.

Goals & Objectives:

  • Increase account onboarding completions by 20%.
  • Simplify quick payments and P2P transfers.
  • Encourage personal savings through automated rounding tools.

2. My Role & Collaboration

As the Lead Mobile UX Designer, I owned user research, wireframing, high-fidelity UI design, prototyping, and micro-interaction definitions.

3. Research & Discovery

We interviewed 15 students and young professionals, discovering they wanted clear, visual feedback of their savings goals rather than standard spreadsheet ledgers.

We established vibrant dark-themed design system guidelines with neon color highlights.

7. High-Fidelity Walkthrough

Interactive Savings Bucket

A progress ring showing custom bucket icons (e.g. Vacation, Gaming PC) that animates dynamically as micro-change is added.

8. Product Strategy

We prioritized mobile UI responsiveness over browser portals, choosing to deploy a mobile application exclusively for our first phase.

9. Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Unclear instant payment confirmation

The Problem: Users were anxious when sending larger amounts because payment feedback screens took up to 3 seconds to update.

Design Solution: We added immediate visual feedback animations showing loading sequences, cutting user anxiety.
