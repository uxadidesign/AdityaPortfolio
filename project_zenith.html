1. Project Overview

Background & Business Context

Zenith is a workforce management SaaS platform used by mid-market enterprises. The legacy interface was heavily siloed, forcing managers to navigate separate sub-portals to run monthly payrolls and approve time sheets.

We aimed to design a unified payroll and attendance dashboard that streamlines operations and decreases employee onboarding friction.

Goals & Objectives:

  • Accelerate new hire onboarding by 35%.
  • Unify monthly payroll approvals in under 3 clicks.
  • Improve feature adoption success to 40%.

9. Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: High payout error rates

The Problem: HR managers frequently calculated salaries incorrectly due to complex bonus logic variables, leading to bank transfer rejections.

Design Solution: We added an inline preview panel showing final breakdowns and calculations, preventing errors before bank transfers.
