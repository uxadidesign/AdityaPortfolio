1. Project Overview

Background & Business Context

Real estate transaction processing is plagued by extensive paperwork, manual review loops, and high contract rejection rates due to formatting errors. Brokers need a fast, foolproof tool to manage listing documents.

Before our portal redesign, agent contract rejection rates were at 24%, wasting weeks in manual verification loops. Agents struggled to track list histories, causing deal blockages.

Goals & Objectives:

  • Minimize transaction submission errors by 15% through clear inline checklist validators.
  • Accelerate listing processing speed by 25%.
  • Unify paperwork tracking inside a single dashboard.

8. Product Strategy & Trade-offs

We chose to restrict file formats to PDF only, simplifying backend file parsing and layout rendering at the expense of user image uploads.

9. Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Unclear listing status states

The Problem: Agents submitted contracts and didn't know if they were under review, approved, or rejected, leading to constant phone support calls.

Design Solution: We added a visual step tracker with color-coded status badges and detailed error annotations, saving weeks of manual email follow-ups.
